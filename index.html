About

Sanjukta Sarkar spent seventeen years at Cognizant leading portfolio governance and delivery across banking, insurance, and consumer goods clients, part of a twenty-five-year career that also includes IBM and Infosys. She now works with banking and financial services organizations navigating AI-enabled transformation, bringing an operating model and accountability lens to a space often treated as a purely technical or policy problem.

Her approach starts with a simple question: who owns this, by name. Governance built on named accountability holds up under regulatory scrutiny in a way that frameworks alone do not.

Our Name

Sahoj is inspired by the Sanskrit concept of Sahaja — that which is natural and effortless — and the Bengali word Sahoj, meaning simple. We believe the best transformations are not the most complicated ones. They are the ones that bring clarity, align people, and simplify execution.
